About Autobahn Solutions

Dhaka's independent specialist for European and premium cars — OEM-grade diagnostics, ECU coding and electrical fault solving from our Tejgaon workshop.

Who we are

Autobahn Solutions Ltd is an independent specialist workshop in Dhaka — not a dealer — focused on European and premium vehicles. We work on BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Land Rover & Range Rover, Jaguar, Volvo and Volkswagen, as well as premium Japanese cars and hybrids including Lexus and Toyota (Prius).

These cars are built around complex electronics, control modules and CAN-bus networks. They need a workshop that understands their diagnostic protocols and coding requirements — not a general garage. That focus is what we do, all day, every day.

What makes us specialists

The work below is what a general garage usually can't credibly do — and it's exactly where we focus.

OEM-grade diagnostics

Manufacturer-level fault-code reading and live data — far beyond a generic OBD scanner.

ECU coding & programming

Module coding, programming, retrofits and feature coding for European vehicles.

Electrical & CAN-bus

Tracing electrical and CAN-bus faults, wiring and module repair that others give up on.

Hybrid battery work

Hybrid system and battery diagnosis and service for Lexus, Prius and other hybrids.

Behind that work: 15+ years of experience, certified technicians, OEM-grade equipment and a warranty on the work we do. Explore our full range on the services page.
