# 07 · Link-Building & Authority Roadmap

*Quality + relevance over quantity. For a premium specialist, a handful of genuinely relevant, trusted links beats 50 directory junk links. 2026-06-02.*

> Correcting the source docs: the "50+ links in 3 months" target encourages low-quality mass submission, which for a premium brand is risk without reward. **Target: a steady stream of relevant, earned links + complete citations. Never buy links or use PBNs.**

---

## 1. Foundation: citations (Months 1–2)
Covered in [06-local-seo-roadmap.md](06-local-seo-roadmap.md) §3 — Bing/Apple Maps, BD business directories, social profiles, automotive listings, all with identical NAP. This is the baseline trust layer; do it first.

## 2. Relevance links: automotive & enthusiast (Months 2–6, ongoing)
The most valuable links for us are **topically relevant** to European/premium cars in BD.

- **Owner & enthusiast communities (BD):** BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Land Rover, Porsche owner groups/forums; classic & luxury car clubs. Become the recommended specialist through genuine help; earn profile/resource links and word-of-mouth citations.
- **Automotive content sites & YouTubers (BD):** car review channels, auto bloggers, import/reconditioned-car dealers' resource pages. Offer expert commentary, repair explainers, collab videos (you already have YouTube @asl_garage).
- **Supplier/partner pages:** OEM-grade tool vendors, parts suppliers, detailers, tyre/alignment shops — reciprocal "trusted partner" links where genuine.
- **Manufacturer/marque directories** or "find a specialist" listings if any exist for BD.

## 3. Local & business links (Months 2–6)
- BD business/startup media and local Dhaka publications (business profile features).
- Chamber of commerce / trade body memberships (if applicable) — membership pages link out.
- Local event sponsorships (car meets) → event pages + recap coverage.

## 4. PR & digital PR (Months 3–9, opportunistic)
You need a *hook* — links follow stories, not press releases about nothing.
- **Newsworthy angles:** "first/leading independent European-car ECU specialist in BD", hybrid-battery reconditioning (sustainability + cost angle), expert takes on the used-import market, data you can publish (e.g. most common faults you see across X cars in Dhaka).
- **Tactics:** expert quotes/HARO-style outreach to BD auto/business journalists; founder thought-leadership; a genuinely useful **linkable asset** (e.g. "Cost of owning a European car in Bangladesh" guide with real data, or a "Dhaka monsoon car-care checklist").
- **Outcome:** earned editorial links from BD news/auto outlets — high authority, high relevance.

## 6. Content-earned links (Months 4+)
The blog clusters and guides ([05-content-roadmap.md](05-content-roadmap.md)) are link bait when genuinely useful. The cost/ownership guides, fault explainers, and hybrid-battery content are the most linkable. Promote them in the communities above.

---

## 7. Guardrails
- ❌ No bought links, link farms, PBNs, comment spam, mass low-quality directory blasts, or exact-match-anchor over-optimisation.
- ✅ Every link should plausibly send a real BD car owner who might book us.
- ✅ Vary anchor text naturally (brand, URL, descriptive). Don't make every link "BMW repair Dhaka".
- ✅ Track every acquired link in a sheet (URL, source, type, anchor, date, follow/nofollow). Mixed follow/nofollow is natural and fine.

## 8. Realistic targets
| Horizon | Citations | Relevant editorial/partner links |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1–2 | 15–25 solid citations (NAP-consistent) | 0–2 |
| Month 3–4 | citation profile complete + maintained | 2–5 quality links |
| Month 5–8 | — | +1–3 quality links/month |
| Month 9–12 | — | digital-PR asset(s) earning ongoing links |

Quality bar per link: *would I be happy for Google to judge my site by the company it keeps here?* If no, skip it.
