# 04 · Keyword Strategy

*Consolidated and de-duplicated from the source workbook + docx, realigned to the European-specialist positioning. 2026-06-02.*

> **Read this first:** the volume numbers carried over from the source workbook are **unvalidated estimates and are almost certainly inflated for the Bangladesh market.** A query like "BMW repair Bangladesh" likely sees *tens*, not hundreds, of searches/month — but each one can be a multi-lakh-taka job. **We prioritise by intent × margin × winnability, not by the spreadsheet number.** Validate real volumes in Google Keyword Planner / Search Console before quoting them.

### TIER 4 — Problem / symptom (top-of-funnel, AI-citable, cluster spokes)
High intent, low competition, perfect for blog spokes that link up to brand/service pillars. These are also what AI assistants quote.

|---|---|

Examples: "cost of maintaining a BMW in Bangladesh", "European vs Japanese cars in Dhaka traffic", "how often should I service my Mercedes", "preparing your car for monsoon in Dhaka", "is it worth importing a used BMW to Bangladesh".

## 5. What we deliberately are NOT targeting (and why)

| Removed keyword | Reason |
|---|---|
| car AC gas refill / AC recharge cheap | Price-shopper magnet; erodes premium positioning; commodity margin |
| car battery service (standalone) | Commodity; brings low-value traffic |
| Honda/Nissan/Mitsubishi standalone repair pages | Off-positioning volume play; dilutes specialist signal (keep only Lexus + Toyota-hybrid as premium/bridge) |
| spare parts / spare parts wholesale | Section already removed from the site; not the business model |
| rickshaw/CNG/commercial-vehicle repair | Not our customer |

> If the business later decides volume Japanese repair *is* a real revenue line, revisit — but launch it on a **separate sub-brand or clearly separated section** so it doesn't dilute the European-specialist entity.

## 6. Validation workflow (do this, don't trust the spreadsheet)

1. Put every Tier 0–3 keyword into **Google Keyword Planner** (BD geo, English+Bengali) and **Search Console** (once pages are live) to get *real* impressions.
2. Re-rank within each tier by *actual* impressions × intent.
3. Mine **GBP "search queries"** and **GA4 site-search** for the words real customers use (often Banglish, e.g. "BMW gari repair Dhaka").
4. Quarterly: prune dead keywords, promote rising ones, add new problem/symptom spokes from real customer questions.
