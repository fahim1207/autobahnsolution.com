Symptoms that mean your Mercedes needs a diagnostic

If your car is showing any of these, a proper scan should come before any parts are touched:

  • Check-engine light — on, flashing, or coming back after a reset.
  • Limp / ECO mode — reduced power or the car refusing to rev past a set limit.
  • Electrical & COMAND faults — infotainment, displays, lighting, sensors or intermittent gremlins.
  • SCR / AdBlue warnings — emission-system messages, countdowns or start-prevention alerts on diesels.
  • ABS, ESP & airbag lights — safety-system warnings that disable features until resolved.

Our Mercedes diagnostic process

Our diagnostics follow a disciplined sequence so nothing gets missed and you don't pay for guesswork:

  • Scan all modules — we read every control unit with OEM-grade equipment, not just the engine, capturing stored and pending codes.
  • Review live data — we watch sensors, actuators and module values in real time while the car runs to see how the fault behaves.
  • Identify the root cause — we interpret the codes and live data together to isolate the actual failure, not just the symptom it triggered.
