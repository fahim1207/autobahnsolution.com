Common Toyota problems we fix

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • Hybrid battery warnings and the red triangle of death — weak or failing cell blocks
  • Hybrid-system and inverter-coolant faults, and reduced fuel economy
  • CVT judder, slip or overheating from neglected fluid
  • AC not cooling — especially electric-compressor systems on hybrids
  • Suspension knocks, worn bushes and steering complaints from rough roads
  • Electrical gremlins — sensors, wiring, charging and check-engine lights

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.

Hybrid battery & diagnostics capability

Hybrid work is where most Dhaka garages fall short. We test hybrid battery packs at cell-block level — measuring voltage and internal resistance to find the weak modules — and check the inverter, hybrid cooling and the high-voltage system properly. That means you get an honest answer on whether your battery needs reconditioning or replacement, instead of guesswork. For dedicated battery work see our hybrid battery repair and Prius hybrid battery pages.

Diagnostics & electrical troubleshooting

Most modern faults are only visible — and only fixable — with proper diagnostic tools and someone who knows how to read them. We trace electrical and CAN-bus faults, sensor and wiring problems, charging issues and persistent check-engine lights back to the actual cause. See our car diagnostics page for how we approach it.
