An Audi is built around tightly integrated electronics — dozens of networked control modules, the MMI infotainment system, and gearboxes like S-tronic that depend on precise software. A general garage simply isn't equipped to read or repair that. Most Audi faults are only visible — and only fixable — with manufacturer-level diagnostic tools and a technician who knows how to interpret them. That's what we do.

Audi services we offer

  • Full diagnostics — fault-code reading and live-data analysis with OEM-grade equipment, not a generic scanner.
  • ECU coding, programming & adaptation — module coding and the adaptations Audis need after repairs.
  • Electrical & MMI fault solving — wiring, sensors, control modules and infotainment, our core strength on European cars.
  • DSG / S-tronic transmission work — mechatronic diagnosis, fault solving and service.
  • quattro driveline repair — diagnosis and repair of all-wheel-drive components.
  • Mechanical repair & scheduled servicing — engine, cooling and suspension work, plus oil, filters and fluids to Audi intervals.

Common Audi faults we fix in Dhaka

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • EPC (Electronic Power Control) warning lights and reduced-power conditions
  • DSG / S-tronic mechatronic problems — rough shifts, hesitation and gearbox fault codes
  • quattro driveline faults affecting traction and drivability
  • Electrical and MMI gremlins — modules, sensors, wiring and infotainment communication faults
  • Oil consumption and timing-related issues on some TFSI engines
  • Coding and adaptation required after replacing batteries, modules or major components

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.
