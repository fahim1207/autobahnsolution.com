Common Mercedes faults we fix in Dhaka

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • Limp / ECO-mode and reduced-power warnings with a check-engine light
  • AdBlue / DEF and SCR system faults on diesels — dosing, NOx sensors and warning countdowns
  • Electrical gremlins, plus SBC braking and AIRMATIC air-suspension faults
  • Cooling-system failures (water pump, thermostat, expansion tank) and corrosion/rust issues — critical in our climate
  • Coding/adaptation required after replacing batteries, modules or major components

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.
