Hybrids are everywhere on Dhaka's roads — but proper hybrid service is rare. A Prius, Aqua, Harrier, Vezel or Grace runs a high-voltage battery, an inverter, a dedicated cooling circuit and regenerative braking, and most general garages simply aren't equipped to diagnose them safely. We are: hybrid service is one of the things we do best, across both Toyota and Honda systems.

Common hybrid problems we fix

  • Hybrid battery warning lights and the dreaded red triangle
  • Sudden drop in fuel economy or loss of EV-mode
  • Weak or failing battery cell blocks

  • Hybrid AC not cooling (electric compressor)

We always diagnose the root cause first — on a hybrid, replacing a whole battery when a few modules are weak is an expensive guess we help owners avoid.

Hybrid battery diagnostics — done properly

The single most valuable thing we do for hybrid owners is an honest battery diagnosis. We measure each cell block's voltage and internal resistance, look at how the pack behaves under load, and check the cooling that keeps it healthy. That tells us whether your pack needs reconditioning, module replacement or a full battery — so you spend on what the car actually needs, not on a guess.

Diagnostics, AC & electrical capability

Hybrids are as much electronics as they are mechanics. We trace electrical and CAN-bus faults, sensor and wiring problems and persistent warning lights back to the actual cause with proper diagnostics, and we handle AC and climate-control repair including the electric-compressor systems hybrids use.

A dealer is expensive and slow; a general garage often can't safely work on a high-voltage hybrid at all. We sit in between: specialist-grade hybrid capability, honest diagnosis, and a clear quote before any work starts — from a team that services European and Japanese cars side by side every day.
