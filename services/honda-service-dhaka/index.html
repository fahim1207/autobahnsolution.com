Common Honda problems we fix

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • Hybrid battery warnings and reduced economy on Vezel, Grace and Fit Hybrid
  • i-DCD dual-clutch judder, jerky shifting and clutch faults
  • CVT judder, slip or overheating from neglected fluid
  • AC not cooling — especially electric-compressor systems on hybrids
  • Suspension knocks, worn bushes and steering complaints from rough roads
  • Electrical gremlins — sensors, wiring, charging and check-engine lights

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.

Hybrid battery & diagnostics capability

Honda's i-DCD hybrids combine a high-voltage battery with a dual-clutch transmission — a system most Dhaka garages can't read properly. We test hybrid battery packs at cell-block level, check the hybrid system and cooling, and diagnose the dual-clutch behaviour so you get an honest answer rather than guesswork. For dedicated battery work see our hybrid battery repair page and our cross-brand hybrid car service.
