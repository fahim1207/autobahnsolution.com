Common Mazda problems we fix

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • i-stop not working — usually battery, sensor or charging-system related
  • SkyActiv carbon build-up, rough running and misfires
  • Oil dilution and oil-level complaints on SkyActiv diesels
  • AC not cooling — compressor, condenser and gas issues
  • Suspension knocks, worn bushes and steering complaints from rough roads
  • Electrical gremlins — sensors, wiring, charging and check-engine lights

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.
