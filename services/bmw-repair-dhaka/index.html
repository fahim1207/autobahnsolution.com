Common BMW faults we fix in Dhaka

Dhaka's heat, traffic and fuel quality are hard on these cars. The issues we see most often:

  • Drivetrain-malfunction / reduced-power and EML (check-engine) warnings
  • Electrical gremlins — modules, sensors, wiring and CAN-bus communication faults
  • Cooling-system failures (water pump, thermostat, expansion tank) — critical in our climate
  • Coding/adaptation required after replacing batteries, modules or major components
  • Rough running, misfires and oil leaks on higher-mileage engines

We always diagnose the root cause first — replacing parts on a guess is how owners end up paying twice.
